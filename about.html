About Us

Excellence in Education Since Inception

About Sethupathy Government Arts College

Sethupathy Government Arts College, Ramanathapuram, is a premier institution of higher learning in the Ramanathapuram District. Established with the vision of providing quality education to the rural and economically backward students of this region, the college has grown into a center of academic excellence.

It is affiliated to Alagappa University and has been accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC, reflecting its commitment to high standards in education and infrastructure. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines.

With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a vibrant campus life, SGAC continues to empower students to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our History

Founded to serve the educational needs of the coastal district of Ramanathapuram, Sethupathy Government Arts College has a rich history of producing distinguished alumni who serve in various capacities across the globe.

The college was established with the noble objective of making higher education accessible to students from rural and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Over the years, it has evolved into a leading institution known for its academic rigor, research contributions, and community engagement.

Today, the college stands as a testament to the transformative power of education, continuing to inspire generations of students to pursue excellence in their chosen fields.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To empower the youth of this region through holistic education, fostering intellectual growth, social responsibility, and ethical values.

Mission

To provide quality education, promote research and innovation, and develop socially responsible citizens who contribute to nation-building.
