IQAC & NAAC

Quality Assurance & Accreditation

NAAC Accredited with 'A' Grade

Sethupathy Government Arts College has been accredited with 'A' Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), reflecting our commitment to maintaining high standards in education, infrastructure, and overall institutional performance.

About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) was established in accordance with the guidelines of NAAC to develop a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.

IQAC acts as a nodal agency for coordinating quality-related activities, including the adoption and dissemination of best practices, organization of workshops, seminars, and feedback collection from stakeholders.

IQAC Objectives

Quality Enhancement

Ensure timely, efficient, and progressive performance of academic, administrative, and financial tasks

Continuous Improvement

Promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement

Stakeholder Engagement

Facilitate creation of learner-centric environment conducive to quality education

Documentation

Maintain proper documentation of various programs and activities

IQAC Functions

  • Development and application of quality benchmarks/parameters for various academic and administrative activities
  • Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education and faculty maturation
  • Arrangement for feedback response from students, parents, and other stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes
  • Dissemination of information on various quality parameters to all stakeholders
  • Organization of workshops, seminars on quality-related themes and promotion of quality circles
  • Documentation of various programs/activities leading to quality improvement
