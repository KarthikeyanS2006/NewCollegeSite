Eligibility Criteria

UG Programs

  • Passed 12th Standard / HSC
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST)
  • Subject-specific requirements apply

PG Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST)
  • Entrance exam may be required

Ph.D. Programs

  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 55% marks (50% for SC/ST)
  • Research entrance test & interview
